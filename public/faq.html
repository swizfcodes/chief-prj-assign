Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Who is a member?

Anyone that has the interest of the town and has a valid income, and is ready to work with others for the development of the town in terms of human capital, infrastructure, and the upgrade of the dynamics of the society.

How do I join?

Pay a minimum of N12,000 (Twelve Thousand Naira) annually to the association account, register on this platform, and attend meetings called either physically or online.

How can I register on this platform?

  • Login with your phone number and password.
  • Use Forgot Password to reset or create a new password.
  • Click on Register to create your profile.

Who are the Admin Users?

These are the current executive committee members. They log in using their email address and password. Admin users cannot create themselves.

I paid but my payment is not showing

Updates are done manually from SMS alerts or the bank statement. There may be a delay if the SMS didn't deliver. Once the statement is reviewed, your payment will be updated. If it's been more than two weeks, please contact any of the officers. You can also paste your payment receipt on the Oyin Sons and Daughters Forum to speed up the process.
