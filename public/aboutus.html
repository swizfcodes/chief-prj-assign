Who We Are

The Oyin-Akoko Community Development Association (OCDA) was established to unite the elites of our community and work closely with His Royal Majesty, Kabiyesi, in achieving holistic and sustainable development for Oyin-Akoko. It serves as a platform that brings together all well-meaning Oyin indigenes to foster collaboration, unity, and coordinated action.

Our Mission

To create a unified structure that coordinates the efforts of all Oyin sons and daughters, drives the growth and development of Oyin-Akoko, and ensures consistent funding for impactful community projects.

Our Vision

To transform Oyin-Akoko into a model town through inclusive development, strategic partnerships, and sustained community empowerment.

Our Core Values

  • Unity: We believe in the strength of collective efforts.
  • Service: We serve with passion and a deep sense of responsibility.
  • Transparency: We uphold openness and accountability in our actions.
  • Commitment: We are dedicated to the long-term development of our town; Oyin-Akoko.

What We Do

  • Mobilize and coordinate community stakeholders
  • Initiate and fund development projects
  • Engage with local authorities and traditional leadership
  • Foster unity and shared identity among Oyin indigenes
  • Promote education, health, and economic empowerment
